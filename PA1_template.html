Loading and preprocessing the data

We are getting the data using \(read.csv\) function. Then we check what are the data columns and values using \(head\) function. We \(aggregate\) to have average values by day and ignore time intervals. We give resulting columns new \(colnames\) using respective funciton. We embelish the dataset with weekday names and numbers using \(format\) and \(wday\) functions.

require(lubridate)
## Loading required package: lubridate
tblload = read.csv("~/git/RepData_PeerAssessment1/activity.csv")

head(tblload)
##   steps       date interval
## 1    NA 2012-10-01        0
## 2    NA 2012-10-01        5
## 3    NA 2012-10-01       10
## 4    NA 2012-10-01       15
## 5    NA 2012-10-01       20
## 6    NA 2012-10-01       25
stpByDay= aggregate(tblload$steps, list(tblload$date), mean, na.action=na.omit)
colnames(stpByDay) = c('date','stpAvg')
dow <- function(x) format(as.Date(x), "%A")
stpByDay$weekDay <- dow(stpByDay$date)
stpByDay$weekDayNum <- wday(stpByDay$date)
table(stpByDay$weekDay)
## 
##    Friday    Monday  Saturday    Sunday  Thursday   Tuesday Wednesday 
##         9         9         8         8         9         9         9

Imputing missing values

Replace missing values with average mean steps.

missingIdx = is.na(stpByDay$stpAvg)

stpByDay$stpAvg[missingIdx] = mean(stpByDay$stpAvg, na.rm=TRUE)
